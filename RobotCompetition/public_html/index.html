Who

The competition is hosted by the THS Robotics Club in cooperation with Timbuktu High School.

What

This is a robot competition! We have three events in which a team can participate: Sprint, Battle, and Maze.

Where

The event will take place at Timbuktu High School. Follow the signs.

When

We're planning the competition for February 30, 2034. Subject to change due to weather, of course.

Why

For fun! Why not?! Also, to introduce robotics to high school students. As our world becomes more and more automated, it is increasingly important to teach upcoming generations about robotics and computing.

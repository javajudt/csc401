Rules

General

  1. Team members must attend the high school.
  2. No more than 3 team members.
  3. Robots must be pre-programmed.
    1. No programming during the contest.
    2. Physical changes (rewiring) are legal during the contest.
    3. Competitors who are not ready at their start time are disqualified.

Robot

  1. Robots can be no wider than 6 inches, and no taller than 12 inches.
  2. Must be self-powered by batter. No gas or solar power!

Event
